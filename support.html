Getting Started with TestFlight

  1. Request Access: Fill out our beta form on the homepage
  2. Receive Invite: We'll send you a TestFlight invitation within 24 hours
  3. Install TestFlight: Download TestFlight from the App Store if you haven't already
  4. Join Beta: Accept the invitation and install Unfinished Work
  5. Start Creating: Begin sharing projects and getting AI insights!

TestFlight Requirements

  • iOS 14.0 or later
  • TestFlight app installed
  • Valid Apple ID
  • Stable internet connection

Beta Testing Guidelines

  • Test Regularly: Use the app daily to help identify issues
  • Report Bugs: Use the feedback form below or email us directly
  • Be Patient: Beta versions may have bugs and performance issues
  • Stay Updated: Install new beta builds when available

Known Beta Issues

  • Performance: Some features may be slower than final release
  • AI Responses: Occasional delays during peak usage
  • Social Features: Following/unfollowing may have temporary sync delays
  • Face ID: May require re-authentication after app updates

üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

App Won't Open

Solution: Force close the app and restart. If the issue persists, reinstall from TestFlight.

Face ID Not Working

Solution: Check Face ID settings in iOS Settings ‚Üí Face ID & Passcode. Ensure the app has permission.

AI Responses Are Slow

Solution: Check your internet connection. AI processing requires a stable connection to OpenAI's servers.

Posts Not Syncing

Solution: Pull down to refresh the feed. Check your internet connection and try logging out and back in.

Can't Follow Other Users

Solution: Ensure you're logged in and have a stable internet connection. Try restarting the app.

üìö Feature Guides

How to Use Unfinished Work

Creating Posts

  1. Tap the "Create" tab
  2. Add text describing your project
  3. Optionally add images
  4. Choose public or private visibility
  5. Tap "Post" to share

Getting AI Insights

  1. Go to the "AI Coach" tab
  2. Choose your mode: Coach, Critic, or Research
  3. Describe your project or current challenge
  4. Tap "Get Insights"
  5. Review and apply the AI's suggestions

Following Other Creators

  1. Go to the "Discover" tab
  2. Search for creators by name or interests
  3. Tap "Follow" on their profile
  4. See their posts in your feed
