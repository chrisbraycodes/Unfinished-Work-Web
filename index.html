Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI coach work?

+

Our AI has 7 modes: Daily Coach (planning), Project Critic (feedback), Research Assistant (tools), Health Score (analysis), Risk Assessment (planning), Timeline Prediction (scheduling), and Priority Matrix (organization). It analyzes your projects and provides personalized insights.

Is my data secure?

+

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and Face ID authentication. Your data is stored securely and never shared without your permission.

Can I follow other creators?

+

Yes! You can search for and follow other creators, see their posts in your feed, and build a network of like-minded project collaborators.

What is AI Visual Generation?

+

Our AI Visual Generation feature uses DALL-E 3 to create project logos, banners, thumbnails, and mood boards. You can choose from multiple artistic styles and add custom prompts to get exactly the visual content you need for your projects.

Can I use this for team projects?

+

Currently, Unfinished-Work is designed for individual creators with social features. Team collaboration is planned for future releases.
