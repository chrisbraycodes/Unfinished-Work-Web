Common Issues & Solutions

App Won't Open

Solution: Force close the app and restart. If the issue persists, reinstall from TestFlight.

AI Responses Are Slow

Solution: Check your internet connection. AI processing requires a stable connection to OpenAI's servers.

Posts Not Syncing

Solution: Pull down to refresh the feed. Check your internet connection and try logging out and back in.

Can't Follow Other Users

Solution: Ensure you're logged in and have a stable internet connection. Try restarting the app.

How to Use Unfinished-Work

Sharing AI Insights & Posts

  1. Get AI insights from your expert guide (coach, critic, health score, etc.)
  2. Create a post to share your AI insights with friends
  3. Add text describing your project or the AI advice you got
  4. Optionally add images
  5. Choose public or private:
    • Public: Friends and followers see it in their feed - they can like and comment
    • Private: Only you can see it
  6. Tap "Post" to share

Note: When you share publicly, your followers see your AI insights and project updates in their feed. They can like and comment on your posts.

Getting AI Insights

  1. Go to the "AI Coach" tab
  2. Choose your mode: Coach, Critic, Research, Health Score, Risk Assessment, Timeline Prediction, or Priority Matrix
  3. Or use AI Chat for conversational assistance with project creation
  4. Describe your project or current challenge
  5. Tap to get insights (tokens will be deducted)
  6. Review and apply the AI's suggestions

Using AI Chat

  1. Open AI Chat from the AI Coach screen
  2. Start a conversation about your projects
  3. The AI remembers your previous conversations and knows about your projects
  4. Ask to create projects, generate tasks, or get guidance
  5. Use voice AI for hands-free interactions

Following, Liking & Commenting

  1. Go to the "Discover" or "Explore" tab
  2. Search for creators by name, username, or what they're building
  3. Tap "Follow" on their profile
  4. See their public posts in your feed - their AI insights, project updates, and progress
  5. Like posts that inspire you by tapping the heart
  6. Comment on posts to give feedback or ask questions

Note: When you follow someone, you'll see their public posts in your feed. You can like and comment on any public post. Their private posts stay private.

Your Social Feed

  1. Go to the "Home" or "Feed" tab
  2. Scroll through posts from creators you follow
  3. See their AI insights, project updates, and progress photos
  4. Like posts by tapping the heart icon
  5. Comment to give feedback or ask questions
  6. Share your own AI insights and project updates

Note: Your feed shows public posts from people you follow. Share your AI insights so your friends can see what your expert guide said about your project.
