Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI coach work?

+

Your AI companion is like having an expert DIY'er who's already done your project countless times. It acts as your Daily Coach who knows what works from experience, Project Critic who's seen it all and gives honest feedback, Research Assistant who knows the best tools and resources, Health Score to see how your project is really doing, Risk Assessment to help you avoid common mistakes, Timeline Prediction based on real experience, Priority Matrix to keep you organized, plus chat, visual generation, and voice AI. It remembers your conversations and knows all your projects, so you can pick up where you left off.

Is my data secure?

+

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure authentication. Your data is stored securely and never shared without your permission.

Can I follow other creators?

+

Absolutely! Follow other DIY creators to see their posts in your feed - their AI insights, project updates, and progress. Like and comment on posts that inspire you, share your own AI insights with friends, and build a network of creators. You can share publicly or keep everything private - it's your space to work on your projects.

What is AI Visual Generation?

+

Our AI Visual Generation feature uses DALL-E 3 to create project logos, banners, thumbnails, and mood boards. You can choose from multiple artistic styles and add custom prompts to get exactly the visual content you need for your projects.

Can I use this for team projects?

+

Unfinished-Work is built for DIY people working on their own projects, with the option to connect with other creators. It's your personal project buddy that helps you stay on track. We're almost ready for beta testing - join the waitlist to be notified when it's available. Team collaboration features are planned for future updates.
